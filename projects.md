# Research Projects

## Ecological Go/NoGo with Social Robots
Development of experimental paradigms using Pepper and NAO robots to assess inhibitory control in children and adults through naturalistic motor tasks.

## Neuromorphic Analysis of Vocal Dynamics
Design of spiking neural network pipelines to analyze children’s vocal behavior using neuromorphic representations and auditory models.

## Manufacturing Action Recognition
Implementation of deep learning models (Graph Neural Networks) for continuous action recognition from skeleton data in industrial environments.

## Biomedical Signal Processing
ECG acquisition and analysis, HRV extraction, and multimodal physiological data processing for behavioral studies.
